Virgin Cucumber Mojito

Description

-Refreshing summer drink, without the headache

-This recipe yields 8 servings

Ingredients

  • 8 cups cold water
  • 1 individual Mojito-flavoured mix
  • 1 cucumber, peeled and sliced
  • 3 cups ice cubes

Steps

Step 1

Stir cold water and beverage mix together in a pitcher until powder is dissolved; add cucumber slices and ice cubes.

Step 2

Serve and enjoy ;
